## Design System Applied

### Color Palette (WCAG AA Compliant)
- **Primary Text**: #0A2540 (deep navy)
- **Secondary Text**: #425466 (slate)
- **Accent Blue**: #0969DA (links, correct items, CTA)
- **Accent Teal**: #00A4B6 (highlights, working status)
- **Error/Broken**: #CF222E (red — ONLY on white backgrounds)
- **Warning**: #9A6700 (dark amber — gaps)
- **Success**: #1A7F37 (forest green — working items)
- **Backgrounds**: #FFFFFF (white), #F6F8FA (light gray)
- **Headers**: #0A2540 (navy background with white text)
- **Borders**: #D0D7DE (light gray)

### Key Design Principles Enforced
✓ **No Color Contrast Issues** — WCAG AA compliant contrast ratios throughout
✓ **Navy Headers** — All title bars use #0A2540 with white text
✓ **White Step Boxes** — Dark text on white backgrounds
✓ **Colored Left Borders** — 4px left border indicates status (red/amber/blue/green)
✓ **Status Pills** — Colored rectangle with white text, never colored text on colored backgrounds
✓ **Light Section Backgrounds** — #F6F8FA for alternating rows
✓ **Red Only on White** — No red-on-red or red-on-orange
✓ **Professional Typography** — Inter font family with proper weight hierarchy

### Typography
- **Titles**: 22px bold, white on #0A2540
- **Section Headers**: 16px semibold, #0A2540
- **Step Text**: 13px regular, #0A2540
- **Subtext**: 11px, #425466
- **Status Pills**: 10px bold, white on colored background

### Component Styles
- **Steps**: White bg, 1px #D0D7DE border, 8px radius, 4px colored left border
- **Sections**: #F6F8FA bg, 1px #D0D7DE border, 12px radius
- **Headers**: #0A2540 bg, 12px radius (top), white text
- **Arrows**: #425466, clean pointed markers
- **Status Pills**: Colored fill, white 10px bold text, 6px radius

## What Was Fixed
| Issue | Before | After |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Color Contrast | Orange on orange, red on red | Navy headers, white boxes, proper contrast |
| Status Indicators | Yellow text on red boxes | White text on colored pills |
| Step Identification | Inconsistent colors | Colored left borders + status pills |
| Readability | Poor contrast on dark backgrounds | Dark text on white/light gray |
| Professional Appearance | Dated styling | Modern SaaS design |

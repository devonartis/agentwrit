# AgentAuth Enterprise License (Summary — Non-binding Overview)

This document provides a high-level summary of the planned commercial licensing terms for AgentAuth Enterprise. It is **not** a license agreement. Final terms will be provided in a separate contract.

## What is AgentAuth Enterprise?

AgentAuth Enterprise is a commercial, closed-source distribution of AgentAuth that may include:

- additional enterprise-only features;
- integrations and plugins;
- support and SLAs; and
- hosted / managed SaaS offerings.

## Relationship to open-source AgentAuth

The core AgentAuth server is available under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPL-3.0). Under AGPL-3.0, you may:

- use AgentAuth internally;
- modify it; and
- deploy modified versions, subject to the AGPL-3.0 requirements.

If you wish to:

- embed AgentAuth in a proprietary product; or
- offer AgentAuth (or a modified version of it) as a hosted / managed service without AGPL-3.0 obligations;

you will need a separate commercial license.

## Commercial licensing

AgentAuth Enterprise will be offered under a commercial license that:

- permits embedding in closed-source products;
- permits offering AgentAuth as a hosted / managed service;
- does not require you to release your own proprietary code under AGPL-3.0; and
- may include additional features and support.
